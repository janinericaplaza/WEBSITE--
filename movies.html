My Favorite Movies

Watching movies is one of my favorite ways to unwind and get inspired. Here are some films I truly love:

1. I love Lizzy (2023)
the film tells the story of a seminarian who meets and falls in love with Lizzy.

2. Descendants of the sun (2016)
is a popular South Korean drama that tells the love story between a special forces officer and a surgeon

3. Descendants (2015)
is an American media franchise centered on a series of Disney Channel musical fantasy films

4. La La Land (2016)
A modern musical that celebrates dreams and romance in the city of Los Angeles, with unforgettable music and performances.

5. Coco (2017)
A heartwarming animated tale about family, tradition, and the magic of music that never fails to make me smile.
